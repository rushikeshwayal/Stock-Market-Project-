Frequently Asked Questions

What is the stock market?

The stock market is a platform where shares of publicly listed companies are bought and sold.

How do I invest in stocks?

To invest in stocks, you typically open a brokerage account, research companies, and place trades through a broker.

What are dividends?

Dividends are payments made by companies to shareholders as a share of the company's profits.

How can I mitigate risks in the stock market?

You can mitigate risks by diversifying your investments, conducting research, and staying informed about market trends.
